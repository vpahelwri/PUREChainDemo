Political & institutional drivers

Policies, regulations, subsidies, and institutional frameworks that create the enabling environment for energy investment. Includes national energy policies, agricultural extension services, and trade regulations.

The presence or absence of functional rural institutions and cooperatives is a critical determinant of whether smallholders can access productive energy services.
